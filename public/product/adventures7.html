Tour Details

Travel with adventure! This is exactly how we planned this trip. We will go trekking in the steepest places along tourist trails, and then we will swim to our heart's content in the sea, snorkel underwater and swim on sea kayaks. It will be a teleportation to the summer.

We will live in comfortable hotels with swimming pools. No tents, there will be only small backpacks for hiking. We promise a sea of impressions!
